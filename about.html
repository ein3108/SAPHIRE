What is SAPHIRE?

The Purpose

SAPHIRE aims to provide an intelligent household interface that can be applied to many future home appliances sans mesure, enabling them to understand your voice command and infer the user goals and tasks. Moreover, a SAPHIRE built device will try to suggest solutions to and assist in performing the user needs while ambiently orchestrating the processes and resources of the household.

By reading QR codes to inform the SAPHIRE system of your household, you will be able to automatically get your microwave ready to defrost frozen food, turn down the heating or air-conditioning, change the lighting, start a vacuum, fill-up a bathtub, and more! This smart system will expand your control, seamlessly integrate your voice commands into your house, making it more intelligent, cozier, and more efficient.

SAPHIRE aims at extending the present home appliances' capabilities and proposing future smart device models. Therefore, we present a cyber space model of actual devices to simulate which both abstracts the current forms and functionalities as well as introduces the imaginative future-forward features of the device. Creativity is the key, enjoy!

The Input and Output

The end user is provided with Google Glass as a front-end application. The user uses Glass to read the QR code of the target household subsystem (such as a microwave) and the target object (a package of frozen chicken), and commands it to do an operation on them ("cook the package of chicken!")
The output of the system is automatic undestanding and orchestration of the user commands and the available resources.
